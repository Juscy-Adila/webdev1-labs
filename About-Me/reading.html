Some questions people ask me about my reading life!

What genre do you read most?

Honestly, poetry and contemporary fiction. I love books that feel personal and emotional.

Do you have a reading goal?

Yes! I want to read at least 12 books this year, one per month. I'm currently on track 🤞

Physical books or e-books?

Physical books all the way! I love the feel of a real book, but e-books are also just so convenient, I end up using them more

Where do you find book recommendations?

Mostly BookTok on tiktok haha, Pinterest, and from friends! I also love browsing in a bookshop and just picking whatever cover catches my eye
